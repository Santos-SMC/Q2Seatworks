Popular Dog Breeds

Labrador Retrievers

A do-it-all breed that requires constant human interaction. It personifies versatility â€” hunting, showing, family, dock diving, tracking, and obedience.

German Shepherds

As a result of their fervent loyalty to their primary caregivers, these dependable friends frequently serve as assistance dogs and police dogs.

Golden Retrievers

With major parts on Full House, Air Bud, and Homeward Bound, this witty species is essentially the movie star of the group.

French Bulldogs

Frenchies need little exercise despite having big personalities. It's understandable why big-eared dogs are also the most popular breed in New York, San Francisco, and Miami, according to the AKC.

Beagles

Beagles, who are curious and playful, will follow their noses wherever they go (and often into trouble).

Poodles

Poodles come in regular, miniature, and toy sizes, although they are all regarded as a single breed and are quite clever. Even if the majority of poodles today serve as household pets, the "poodle clip" is designed to protect the joints and organs in cold water.
